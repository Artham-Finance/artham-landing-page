Delete Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Artham Finance account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

1
Send an email to support@un-maze.com
2
Use the subject line Delete My Account - Artham Finance
3
Include your registered email address This helps us locate and verify your account before deletion.

We will process your request and delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of receiving your email.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile and login credentials
  • All financial records and transactions
  • Uploaded documents and attachments

Legal retention notice: Some data may be retained for legal and compliance purposes for up to 90 days after your deletion request is processed.
