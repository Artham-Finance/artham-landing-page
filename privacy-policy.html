Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 May 2026

Artham ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Artham mobile application (Android and iOS) and the website at artham.app (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and business name to create and manage your account.

Financial data. To provide the Service, we process the financial documents and data you provide, including:

  • Vendor invoices and bills (photographed, forwarded, or uploaded)
  • Bank statements (PDF or CSV uploads)
  • Invoice and payment records you create within the app
  • Party (customer/vendor) details such as names, GST numbers, and contact information

Device and usage data. We automatically collect device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and general usage analytics to improve the Service.

Camera and storage access. The app requests camera access to photograph bills and invoices, and storage access to upload documents. These permissions are used solely for the stated purpose.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service — including bill extraction, bank statement matching, transaction categorisation, and dashboard reporting
  • To create and manage your account
  • To send transactional communications (e.g., payment reminders, overdue alerts)
  • To improve the Service, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and regulatory requirements

3. AI and Automated Processing

Artham uses artificial intelligence to extract data from invoices and bills, categorise transactions, match payments to invoices, and detect duplicates. All AI-processed data is derived solely from documents and information you provide. We do not use your financial data to train general-purpose AI models.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or financial data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers — trusted third parties who help us operate the Service (cloud hosting, OCR processing, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits. While no system is completely secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Financial records are retained as required by applicable tax and corporate laws (typically 8 years under Indian regulations). You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@un-maze.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

9. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy within the app and on our website, with the updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
